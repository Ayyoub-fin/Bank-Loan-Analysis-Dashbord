Key Highlights
Real-Time Lending Overview
Analyse the total volume of loan applications and funding disbursed. This section displays the specific number of applications and the total capital received from borrowers.

Portfolio Health Breakdown
Perform rapid comparisons between "Good" and "Bad" loans to help customise debt recovery and credit risk strategies.

Financial Insights
Investigate interest rate trends and Debt-to-Income (DTI) ratios to determine the affordability of loans and identify where financial stress peaks.

Operational Performance Tracker
Track Month-to-Date (MTD) and Month-over-Month (MoM) growth for applications and funding to monitor the expansion of lending operations.

📊 What You’ll Find
Portfolio Quality Profiles
Doughnut charts illustrate the split between Good Loans (86.2%) and Bad Loans (13.8%). This data is used to identify segments requiring stricter lending criteria.

Geographic Distribution
A map visualises total funded amounts across different states. Use this to direct resources to regions with the highest lending impact.

Temporal Funding Trends
A line chart indicates the total funded amount by month, highlighting growth patterns and seasonal shifts in borrowing.

Loan Purpose & Term Counts
Bar charts and tables provide a census of loan purposes, terms (36 vs 60 months), and employment lengths to show which borrower profiles are most common.

Risk Patterns
Slicers for loan "Grade" (A-G) allow for a segmented view of interest rates and DTI ratios across different risk categories.

Application Timing
KPI cards show application volume by month, including MTD and MoM overlays. This allows you to see high-growth periods immediately.
